# 🚗 Car Insurance Claims Analysis

## 📌 Business Context
Accidents occur worldwide due to factors such as negligent driving, adverse climatic conditions, and infrastructure issues. These incidents lead to a significant number of insurance claims, posing challenges for insurance companies.

Accurate estimation of claims is crucial for:
- Business sustainability and profitability  
- Financial reserve optimization  
- Development of innovative insurance policies  

Analyzing claims data helps in:
- Identifying trends and patterns  
- Detecting fraud  
- Improving customer segmentation  
- Enabling dynamic pricing based on risk profiles  

---

## 🎯 Objective
As a **Lead Data Analyst**, the goal of this project is to:

- Explore the Car Insurance Claims dataset  
- Identify patterns and trends  
- Present findings through an **interactive storyboard**  
- Deliver **actionable insights for Senior Management**  

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Key Questions Addressed

#### 1. Age Distribution of Car Owners
- No significant outliers found  
- Increasing trend until mid-range ages, followed by decline  

#### 2. Income vs Education
- Average income: **66,298**  
- Higher education levels (Masters, PhD) show significantly higher income  

#### 3. Car Age vs Claim Frequency
- Claim frequency increases up to 8 years of car age  
- Declines after 8 years  
- Exceptionally high claims observed for 1-year-old cars  

#### 4. Claim Amount vs Car Use & Gender
- Private cars: Higher total claims than commercial  
- Female drivers: Higher claims compared to male drivers  

#### 5. Car Type vs Car Value
- **Panel Trucks and Vans** have higher average book value  
- No clear relationship between marital status and car type

## 💡 Business Insights & Recommendations
